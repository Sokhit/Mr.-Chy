About me

My name is Chy, I am a local Cambodian driver with over 5 years of experience. I was born in a countryside in the eastern part of Siem Reap. I am now married with a daughter and 2 cats (my wife considers our cats as our children too :D) I am a quiet but honest person. I am willing to provide the best and safe driving service to all guest regardless of nationalities, religions, genders, colors or ethnicities.  

I can offer you a great driving service to visit a lot of wonderful and interesting places that include Angkor Wat temple complex and many other sights and areas that are worth seeing. Apart from all the fun and adventure I can offer you a well-informed Tuk Tuk travel experience with a true local and resident of Siem Reap! I will do my very best to take care of you.  

See you soon! 
Chy
